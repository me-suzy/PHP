Navigating Albums

Navigating albums is easy. The are two navigation toolbars at the top and bottom of a main album view. The main album view is split up into pages. The limits on this toolbar control how many thumbnails to show per page. There are arrows (<< and >>) provided for moving back and forth from page to page. Also page links are there to help jump around from page to page. In order to view a photo all you must do is click on the thumbnail image. This will take you to a big view of that one specific image. Located at the top and bottom of the image will be <<Prev and Next>> links to page through each individual photo. At any time you can get back to the main album view by clicking the Back to Album link at the top of the page.
