SETUP / INSTALACION


1) Open in your text editor the file floodconfig.php and change anything you need.
2) Upload everything to your site and CHMOD dataflood.db to 777.
3) To insert the shout into your webpages use this code:

<iframe frameborder=0 src="http://www.yourdomain.com/shoutboxfolder/floodbox.php" width="200" height="500"> </iframe>

Of course you must change the source (src), width and the height according to your site ;).
